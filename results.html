Profile Achievements explained:


Competitors can unlock 'Achievements' that get displayed on their profile.
These cover mainly results based achievements, but not exclusively.


The badges that can be earned or displayed (right now) are:


  • Championship Podiums
  • Records
  • Career Length
  • Medals won
